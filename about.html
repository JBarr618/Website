🚦 About Signal School 2026

Real Railroad Signal Training

Signal School 2026 was created to provide real-world railroad signal training built from field experience in maintenance, testing, troubleshooting, and engineering.

The goal is to help signal maintainers, technicians, engineers, and students understand railroad signaling systems using practical explanations and professional training modules.

Topics Covered

  • Track Circuits
  • Switches
  • Interlockings
  • Relays
  • CBTC Systems
  • Signal Troubleshooting
  • Field Maintenance
